Frequently Asked Questions

What does Finmee Technologies do?

Finmee Technologies, operating under the brand NTWIST, specializes in artificial intelligence and machine learning solutions to automate business processes and help industries make data-driven decisions.

Where is Finmee Technologies located?

Finmee Technologies is based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It operates from both Domalguda and the IIIT-Hyderabad Gachibowli campus.

What industries does Finmee serve?

Finmee primarily serves industrial sectors by applying AI to reduce downtime, improve maintenance planning, and boost operational efficiency across manufacturing, energy, and related domains.

Do they offer international services?

Yes, Finmee Technologies serves clients globally, offering end-to-end digital transformation solutions with AI-driven insights and predictive analytics.

How can I contact Finmee Technologies?

You can reach them through their website at www.ntwist.com or visit their Hyderabad offices listed on official directories.

Is Finmee Technologies a startup?

Yes, Finmee Technologies is a deep-tech startup founded in 2016, focusing on cutting-edge AI and ML innovations.
